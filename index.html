Thanks for visiting my site.

I am Sushma Kumari, a Frontend Developer with 8 years of experience in the IT industry. My journey began as a JavaScript app developer, and I soon transitioned to working with Angular, starting with Angular 1.x and progressively moving to higher versions such as Angular 15. I also had the opportunity to work with React for a year, which broadened my skills and adaptability in frontend technologies.

My career commenced at CGI, where I honed my skills in requirement analysis, development, testing, maintenance, and support. Following CGI, I moved on to work with Virtusa, Mindtree, and NTT Data, each transition marking a significant step in my professional growth. In these roles, I continuously upgraded my technical expertise and embraced new challenges, contributing to various large-scale projects and enterprise applications.

Driven by a desire to blend technical proficiency with leadership, I pursued a Project Management course to enhance my skills in both technical and managerial domains. This education has empowered me to thrive in the industry, allowing me to explore team and product management alongside my core technical work.

Throughout my career, I have remained committed to continuous learning. I have delved deeply into frontend development, focusing on aesthetics, accessibility, responsive design, performance, and optimization. Coding and experimenting with CSS have been a daily passion, reflecting my dedication to creating visually appealing and high-performing web applications.

The ever-evolving nature of the web industry has kept me on my toes, always striving for improvement. I have worked with various technologies, solved complex problems, mentored teammates, and served as a Subject Matter Expert (SME). My journey has been a roller coaster, filled with diverse projects, relocations, and cultural experiences, particularly relocating to Canada and embracing its culture and work environment.

I am excited about the prospect of collaborating with you to create modern, responsive websites. Let's connect and bring innovative ideas to life!
